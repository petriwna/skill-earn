What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us improve user experience and analyze traffic.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • - Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality.
  • - Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user interactions and improve the site.
  • - Preference Cookies: Store your settings and preferences.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may impact your experience on the site.
